# Intake Checklist

## Required Inputs

| Field | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Location (park/area) | Determines regulations, hazards, and weather patterns |
| Dates or season | Drives temperature, daylight, and crowd levels |
| Nights | Sets food, water, and schedule scope |
| Group details (count, ages) | Impacts gear sizing, pace, and safety limits |
| Experience level | Prevents over-scoping activities |
| Campsite mode | Changes gear, water, and toilet assumptions |
| Transportation | Controls loading limits and access |
| Gear owned | Avoids duplicate recommendations |
| Budget | Prioritizes buy/rent/borrow recommendations |
| Dietary and medical needs | Prevents unsafe meal plans |
| Activities desired | Shapes itinerary and equipment |

## Follow-Up Questions (Ask Only Missing Items)

1. What exact campground or zone are you targeting?
2. What dates are you going, or what month/season?
3. Who is going (adults, kids, ages)?
4. Is this car camping, walk-in, dispersed, or RV?
5. What camping gear do you already own?
6. Any dietary restrictions, allergies, medications, or mobility limits?
7. What weather are you willing to camp in?
8. What is your budget range for new gear and food?

## Defaults Policy

Use defaults only after stating assumptions:
- Experience: beginner
- Budget: moderate
- Campsite type: established campground
- Activity intensity: easy
- Cooking method: camp stove + no-cook backup

When assumptions are used, include a short "Assumptions used" note at the top of the plan.
